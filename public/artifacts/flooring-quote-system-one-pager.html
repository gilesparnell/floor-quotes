Investor one-pager

A quote-ready sales and operations assistant for flooring installers.

Floor Quotes helps homeowners move from interest to a structured quote request faster, while giving installers the information they need to qualify, price, approve, order, and plan the job.

Positioning

Not another flooring visualizer. The wedge is turning room evidence and product intent into quote-ready project data.

Business problem

Flooring purchases are high-consideration decisions. Customers struggle to imagine how products will look, understand price differences, and know whether a quote is realistic.

Installers lose time answering vague enquiries, visiting homes with poor buying intent, and manually preparing estimates from incomplete information.

Target users

Installers

Independent flooring installers, small retailers, renovation businesses, and trade operators that want better-qualified leads.

Homeowners

People planning floor replacement or renovation who need product confidence, rough pricing, and clearer next steps before committing.

Core workflow

  • Customer enters room details, uploads photos or scans, and chooses flooring preferences.
  • The system estimates material quantity, waste, underlay, trims, labour assumptions, complexity, and likely duration.
  • The installer reviews the lead, adjusts assumptions, sends a polished quote, books a visit, or recommends alternatives.
  • Once approved, the quote becomes the source of truth for ordering materials and planning the work.

Value proposition

For customers

Easier product comparison, faster rough pricing, more confidence in colour and material choice, and clearer expectations after quote approval.

For installers

Better qualified enquiries, faster quoting, stronger online sales experience, clearer records, simpler ordering, and better job-duration estimates.

Monetisation

  • Monthly SaaS subscription for small flooring installers.
  • White-label website plugin for existing installer sites.
  • Setup and catalogue onboarding fees.
  • Premium quote automation for multi-room jobs, stairs, reports, and customer-ready PDFs.
  • Supplier partnerships, referral revenue, or ordering workflow margin where commercially appropriate.

A successful version does not need to replace large retailer platforms. It can win by serving independent installers who want a professional, affordable, practical tool that helps convert more enquiries into paid work.
