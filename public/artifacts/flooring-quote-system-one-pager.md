# Flooring Quote Automation System

## Concept

A digital sales and quoting system for flooring installers that helps homeowners move from interest to quote-ready enquiry faster.

The customer scans or describes their room, explores real flooring products, compares colours and finishes, and receives an indicative quote range. The installer receives a structured lead with room details, selected products, photos, measurements, budget signals, and any complexity notes needed to follow up professionally.

The goal is not to build another flooring visualizer. The goal is to help small flooring businesses sell better, quote faster, reduce wasted site visits, and give customers more confidence before they commit.

## Business Problem

Flooring purchases are high-consideration decisions. Customers often struggle to imagine how products will look in their home, understand price differences, or know whether a quote is realistic. Installers spend time answering vague enquiries, visiting homes with poor buying intent, and manually preparing estimates from incomplete information.

This creates friction on both sides:

- customers delay decisions because they cannot visualise the outcome or estimate cost;
- installers lose time on unqualified leads;
- product selection and quoting are disconnected;
- small installers struggle to present a polished digital experience compared with larger retailers.

## Target Users

- Independent flooring installers
- Small flooring retailers
- Renovation and home-improvement businesses
- Trade businesses that want better qualified leads
- Homeowners planning floor replacement or renovation

## Core Use Cases

### Customer Enquiry

A homeowner starts on the installer’s website, enters basic room details, uploads photos or scans the space, selects the type of flooring they are interested in, and receives an indicative quote range.

### Product Discovery

Customers browse real products from manufacturers and suppliers, filtering by material, colour, price range, durability, finish, and style.

### Visual Confidence

Customers can see product colours, plank styles, finishes, and room-level previews so they feel more confident before requesting a quote.

### Quote Preparation

The system estimates material quantity, waste allowance, underlay, trims, labour assumptions, and complexity factors before passing the lead to the installer.

### Installer Lead Review

The installer receives a clear lead summary with selected products, room details, photos, likely scope, and customer budget expectations.

### Follow-Up And Conversion

The installer can adjust the estimate, send a polished quote, book a site visit, or recommend alternative products.

### Quote Approval

Once the customer approves a quote, the project moves from sales enquiry into job preparation. The approved quote becomes the source of truth for selected products, quantities, labour assumptions, and customer expectations.

### Product And Material Ordering

The installer can order the required flooring products, underlay, trims, adhesives, profiles, and other materials directly through the website or connected supplier workflow. The system helps reduce missed items by linking the order list to the approved quote.

### Job Duration Estimate

Based on room size, flooring type, preparation work, stairs, trims, and installation complexity, the system gives the installer an indicative estimate of how many days the job is likely to take.

This helps with scheduling, customer expectation setting, labour planning, and deciding whether the job requires one installer, a small team, or multiple visits.

## Value Proposition

For customers:

- easier product comparison;
- faster rough pricing;
- more confidence in colour and material choice;
- less uncertainty before speaking to an installer.
- clearer expectations once the quote is approved, including likely timing and next steps.

For installers:

- better qualified enquiries;
- faster quoting;
- reduced time wasted on low-intent leads;
- more professional online sales experience;
- stronger product upsell opportunities;
- clearer records of customer preferences and project scope.
- simpler material ordering from approved quotes;
- fewer missed products or accessories;
- better job-duration estimates for scheduling and labour planning.

## Monetisation Options

### Monthly SaaS Subscription

Charge flooring installers a monthly fee to use the quoting and lead-capture system on their website.

Example tiers:

- Basic: product catalogue, quote form, lead capture.
- Pro: visual previews, quote automation, installer dashboard.
- Premium: multi-user access, branded reports, analytics, supplier catalogue support.

### White-Label Website Plugin

Offer the system as an embeddable branded tool for flooring businesses that already have a website.

### Per-Lead Fee

Charge a fee for each qualified lead generated through the platform, especially if the system becomes a marketplace or directory.

### Setup And Catalogue Onboarding

Charge an upfront setup fee to load supplier products, pricing rules, branding, quote templates, and installer-specific labour assumptions.

### Premium Quote Automation

Offer advanced quoting features as paid add-ons, such as multi-room estimates, stair complexity, product comparison reports, or customer-ready PDF proposals.

### Supplier Partnerships

Partner with flooring manufacturers or distributors who want their products surfaced inside installer quote workflows.

### Ordering Margin Or Referral Revenue

If product ordering is connected through the installer website, the platform could support supplier referral fees, margin on product orders, or preferred-supplier arrangements where commercially appropriate.

### Scheduling And Operations Add-On

Offer paid job-planning features for approved quotes, including material order tracking, job-duration estimates, installer scheduling, and customer status updates.

## Why This Could Work

The product sits at the point where customer intent, product selection, quoting, ordering, and job planning meet. That is where small flooring businesses currently lose time and customers lose confidence.

A successful version does not need to replace large retailer platforms. It can win by serving independent installers who want a professional, affordable, practical tool that helps them convert more enquiries into paid work.

## Positioning Statement

> A quote-ready sales and operations assistant for flooring installers: helping customers visualise products, understand likely costs, approve quotes, order materials, and plan the job with more confidence.
